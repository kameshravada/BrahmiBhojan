# 29 - Future Roadmap

Status: Draft for approval

## Phase 1 - Foundation

Documentation, repo setup, backend skeleton, frontend skeleton, Docker local services, CI baseline.

## Phase 2 - MVP Commerce

Catalog, search, guest cart, OTP login, checkout, Razorpay, order creation, basic admin, inventory.

## Phase 3 - Operations

Delivery partner tracking, notifications, coupons, returns, payment reconciliation, reports, audit logs.

## Phase 4 - Growth

Marketing automation, customer segmentation, advanced analytics, reviews, CMS, campaigns.

## Phase 5 - Scale

Multi-brand enablement, subscriptions, loyalty, advanced delivery integrations, warehouse/multi-location inventory, recommendation engine.

## Phase 6 - Enterprise Expansion

Native apps, marketplace readiness, ERP integrations, event-driven modules, selective service extraction.
