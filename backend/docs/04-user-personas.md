# 04 - User Personas

Status: Draft for approval

## Purpose

Define the primary people who will use, operate, and benefit from BrahmiBhojan.

## Customer Personas

### P1 - Health-Conscious Household Buyer

Needs authentic staples, millets, oils, pulses, dry fruits, and wellness products. Values trust, quality, clear product details, reviews, and repeat ordering.

Success means finding reliable products quickly and receiving them without quality issues.

### P2 - Traditional Food Buyer

Looks for pickles, spices, rice varieties, regional foods, and products connected to family food habits. Values authenticity, taste, origin, and freshness.

Success means discovering traditional products that feel trustworthy and familiar.

### P3 - Urban Convenience Shopper

Wants quick search, simple cart, online payment, and predictable delivery. Values mobile-first flows and minimal login friction.

Success means completing an order in minutes without password or account friction.

### P4 - Monthly Grocery Planner

Buys staples in larger quantities. Values price clarity, pack sizes, availability, offers, and order history.

Success means building a reliable monthly basket.

## Admin Personas

### P5 - Founder/Super Admin

Needs complete visibility into revenue, orders, customers, operations, campaigns, and system health. Can access all admin areas.

### P6 - Catalog Admin

Manages products, categories, images, descriptions, SEO content, visibility, and pricing inputs.

### P7 - Operations Admin

Manages packing, delivery partner handoff, order status updates, cancellations, returns, and exception handling.

### P8 - Marketing Admin

Manages coupons, campaigns, customer segments, notifications, and automation workflows.

### P9 - Finance Admin

Monitors payments, refunds, Razorpay references, GST-level cart data, reconciliation, and reports.

## Persona Design Rules

- Customer UX must be mobile-first.
- Admin UX must be dense, searchable, permission-aware, and audit-friendly.
- No customer should be forced to create a password.
- Support rural and urban addresses.
